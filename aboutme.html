About Me


Hi, I’m Amelia!

I’m a UX/UI designer, color designer, trend forecaster, and surface pattern designer. My signature style is at once minimal, colorful, joyful, and strikes a balance of timelessness and trend-aware style.

My UX/UI skills focus on qualitative research and insights, user testing, prototyping and UI design strategy that drives emotional connection and brand appeal.

In the retail space, I specialize in collaborating to develop brand visualization, strategy, color palette and print direction for owned across home, tech, travel, sporting goods, pets, toys, and apparel.

My biggest passion is helping individuals and small businesses to develop their personal brand strategies across style positioning, color, print + graphics, and type.
